The Alchemy Lab

New Project

Discover The Alchemy Lab, where creativity is transformed into magic. I blend design, code, and artistry to craft unique, functional creations. Step in and discover how I can turn your ideas into something extraordinary.

Kana Burger Bar — Brand Logo

For this project, I created an illustrative logo for Kana Burger Bar, featuring the brand name "Kana" integrated into the center of a burger bun. The logo uses a bold black and yellow color scheme to evoke a sense of energy and freshness, aligning with the vibrant and modern identity of the burger bar. Using Adobe Illustrator, I designed this logo to be both playful and memorable, ensuring it works seamlessly across various brand materials, from menus to signage.

Kimi Coffee House — Brand Logo

I had the pleasure of collaborating with Mas Rio and Mba Ica, the owners of Kimi Coffee House, to create a custom sticker pack that embodies the charm and warmth of their café. The pack features four unique designs, each representing different aspects of the brand. The first sticker showcases Kimi the cat, the café's beloved mascot, in a cute and playful illustration. The second design features a classic coffee cup, symbolizing the heart of the café's offerings. The third and fourth stickers both feature the café's tagline, "Kimi Tiap Hari" (meaning "Kimi Every Day"), with the third design set against a bold black background and the fourth on a clean white background, each complemented by an illustration of Kimi the cat.

Goldentimes Club — 3D Modeling

Collaborated with Mas Fiqar and Mas Prama, the owners of Goldentimes Club, to design a semi-permanent store inspired by the street-side shops of the UK. The design features a central door with two large windows, which were divided into four smaller frames due to budget constraints. The facade is made of redwood-colored wood, adding warmth and rustic appeal to the shop. Using SketchUp, I crafted a 3D model that balances aesthetic appeal with practicality, creating a cozy and inviting space that fits within the budget while capturing the classic, timeless feel of a quaint street-side shop.

Onde Onde Ibu — Brand Logo

When designing the 'Onde Onde Ibu' logo, I wanted to keep it simple yet meaningful. The circular shape, the main element of the logo, was directly inspired by the onde-onde itself. This perfect circle symbolizes the simplicity and authenticity of the onde-onde's taste, which has been enjoyed by generations

Self Project — 3D Modeling

I created a 3D model of a house inspired by the unique illustration style of @Kazelene. Using Blender, I translated the vibrant and whimsical elements of the illustration into a detailed 3D structure, capturing the essence of the original artwork while adding depth and dimension. The model showcases a blend of playful architectural features and stylized textures, reflecting the creative and imaginative spirit of @Kazelene's art.

Ikatiket — 3D Modeling

This self-initiated project involved replicating the interior design of the Ikatiket store as a way to learn and practice 3D modeling in Blender. I focused on accurately recreating the store’s layout, paying attention to details such as furniture, lighting, and textures. The goal was not to conceptualize a new design but to develop my skills in modeling, material application, and rendering.
